About Us

Climate Change for Pastoral Community is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) dedicated to addressing climate change issues affecting pastoral communities in Garissa County. Our mission is to raise awareness, promote sustainable practices, and empower local communities to adapt to and mitigate the effects of climate change.

Our Projects

Tree Planting Initiative

Implement a tree planting initiative in Garissa County to combat deforestation and mitigate the effects of climate change.

Solar Energy Access Program

Launch a solar energy access program to promote renewable energy adoption and reduce reliance on fossil fuels in Garissa County.

Research

Study on Climate Trends

A comprehensive analysis of climate data to identify trends and patterns in temperature, rainfall, and extreme weather events in Garissa County.

Impact Assessment Report

An assessment report on the impact of climate change on local communities, ecosystems, agriculture, and livelihoods in Garissa County.
